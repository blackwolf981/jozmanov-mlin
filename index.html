Zgodovina in dediščinaHistory & heritage

Sedem vodnih kolesSeven water wheels

Jožmanov mlin izvira iz 18. stoletja in je omenjen že v Franciscejskem katastru leta 1824. Njegova posebnost je bila tehnično napredna zasnova s kar sedmimi podlivnimi vodnimi kolesi — skoraj industrijska stopnja obdelave žita za tisti čas.The Jožman Mill dates from the 18th century and is recorded in the Franciscean cadastre of 1824. Its distinguishing feature was an advanced design with as many as seven undershot water wheels — an almost industrial scale of grain processing for its time.

Skupaj s kamnito kaščo, vklesano v živo skalo, jezom in mostom tvori redko ohranjeno celoto, kjer se prepletajo tehnična dediščina, tradicionalna arhitektura in naravna krajina.Together with the stone granary carved into living rock, the weir and the bridge, it forms a rare, intact ensemble where technical heritage, traditional architecture and natural landscape intertwine.
